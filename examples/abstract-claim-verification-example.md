# Abstract Claim Verification Example

This example is synthetic. It does not describe an unpublished manuscript or private research finding.

## Draft sentence

> AI-assisted research is now accepted by major scientific publishers.

## Claim ledger entry

| ID | Claim | Source support | Evidence quality | Confidence | Status | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| C-001 | Major publishers increasingly permit some AI-assisted research or writing tasks under conditions such as disclosure and human responsibility. | S-001, S-002 | Medium | High | Revise | "Accepted" hides the conditions. |

## Source log

| ID | Source | Type | Date accessed | Relevance | Limits |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| S-001 | ICMJE, AI Use by Authors | Policy | 2026-06-30 | Sets human accountability and disclosure expectations. | Medical-journal focused. |
| S-002 | Elsevier generative AI policies | Publisher policy | 2026-06-30 | Allows some AI use with restrictions. | Publisher-specific. |

## Revised sentence

> Major publishers increasingly permit some AI-assisted research and writing tasks, but typically require human responsibility, source checking and disclosure.

## Decision

The original sentence was too broad. The revised sentence keeps the trend and makes the conditions visible.
