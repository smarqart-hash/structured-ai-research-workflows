## Field guide

- Claim: one statement a reviewer can check.
- Source support: source IDs or links.
- Evidence quality: strength of the source base.
- Confidence: author's confidence after review.
- Status: keep, revise or drop.
- Notes: qualifiers, caveats or follow-up checks.
